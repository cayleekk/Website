About me

Hi! I'm Caylee Cumberbatch-Carter, a 16-year-old sophomore in high school and a proud member of the Class of 2028. I hold a 3.7 GPA and I'm committed to growing both academically and as a person.

I love working with children and find it incredibly rewarding to make a difference in their lives. On the athletic side, I run track and enjoy the discipline and energy that comes with being part of a team. When I'm not on the track or out in the community, I enjoy studying — yes, I genuinely like learning!

My volunteer experience at the MSPCA and Mass General Brigham has shaped who I am and showed me the value of showing up for others.
